SOFTSTEP / 12STEP

Both SoftStep and 12Step are Multi-dimensional MIDI foot controllers. It was the second product at KMI we patented using our fabric sensor technology. While SoftStep is for the advanced pedal-er, 12Step is more akin to legacy foot switches.
